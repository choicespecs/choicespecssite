Who am I?

Hi, I'm Christopher Lee.

You've come across my own personal portfolio page. The name of the site comes from one of my favorite video games. It's a special item that increases an attribute called Special Attack at the cost of being limited to using the same attack. However; real life requires you to be much more flexible and you always need multiple tools, skills, and knowledge to be successful. If I had to be limited to one specific kind of attack or area, It would be full stack development which requires you to have experience in all fields.

My expertise is mainly in website development and currently working on learning and developing web applications as time continues. I can work in a multiple amount of different languages such as C, C++, Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. However; I would rather let my experience and projects speak for themselves. If you would be interested in working together or have any questions, feel free to reach out!

Experience in everything in front-end to back-end development. I can do everything from developing websites to web apps from conception to full development. I am always seeking new opportunities to both grow and learn within the field. If interested, feel free to reach out!
